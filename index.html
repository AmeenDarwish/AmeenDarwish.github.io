Welcome to the Hebrew University Research Computing Services

As of 2021, the Hebrew University provides centralized research computing services (RCS) to all members of the Hebrew University, as well as users from other universities.

 

The services are designed to lower computation costs and to free researchers and academic units from the burden of maintaining independent computational facilities. By implementing a modern cooling design, the centralized computing services are also a green step forward.   

 

RCS provides local cloud-like services for all types of computations, such as:

- Processing on CPU nodes

- Processing on GPU nodes

- Server and web services 

- Data storage and backup

- Software development

 

RCS provides technical support and assistance in optimising the execution of various software applications, such as: 

- Numerical simulations

- Image processing 

- Machine learning algorithms

- Big Data analysis

- Statistical computations
